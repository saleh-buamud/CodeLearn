Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now". You'll be guided through the registration process.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied, you can request a full refund within 30 days of enrollment.

Are the courses self-paced?

Our courses are designed to be flexible. While we provide suggested timelines, you can learn at your own pace and access materials 24/7.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile or resume.
